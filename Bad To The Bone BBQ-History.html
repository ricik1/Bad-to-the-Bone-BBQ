This family owned business really started in the back yard of my grandparents place years before I new what BBQ was. However at the time it was a place for the family to gather and catch up on the local gosip and talk about market prices. Everyone brought their favorite seaonings, including dry rubs and homemade sauces. It was always a day of fun and mostly in the sun. We would make baked beans, potato salad, coleslaw and dessert while the meat smoked. The kids had plenty of outdoor games like corn hole, horseshoes and fishing til called in for dinner or the sun went down. As I grew older I took an interest in cooking. I learned the "hand me down recipes" from my grandparents, parents, aunts, uncles and anyone else who was willing to share. I enjoyed it so much I would dream about opening a place one day, where people could gather to gosip, talk about market prices, and relax with home made BBQ made from my heart and the love from my fmaily.
