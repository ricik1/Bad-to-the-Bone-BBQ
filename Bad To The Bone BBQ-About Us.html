Located in Pawnee, Oklahoma, Bad to the Bone was established in 2001 owned and operated by Erica Egan. BBQing has been a family tradition that started in the back yard at family functions. With three brothers and more cousins than can be counted family functions were always large. From a young age Erica could be found helping build the fire and seasoning the meats, learning perfection from her father.
Bad to the Bone BBQ is serving Oklahoma with Texas style BBQ - the tradition of smoking meats the old fashion way. Front and center is our slow smoked USDA Prime brisket. Other popular selections are racks and half racks of beef back ribs, pork ribs and baby back ribs. We use dry rubs and regional woods to bring out the best flavors. We do not use any electric or gas with our smokers. We stive to buy all our products locally to support our local businesses.
We offer a down home, laid back, rustic atmosphere with indoor and outdoor dinning. Large screen tv's placed throughout the indoor dinning area. A full service, wood carved, rustic bar with daily drink specials. Our store proudly supports the local schools, churches and community programs.


  1. Family owned and Operated since 2001
  2. Serve the best quality BBQ in the area
  3. Dedicated to supporting our locals
