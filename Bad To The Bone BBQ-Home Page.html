Bad to the Bone BBQ is located right of the 412 Turnpike & HWY 99, Hallett OK. This is a family owned and operated restaurant, we have been in business for 20 years. We are known for our award-winning slow cooked, wood smoked brisket, pulled pork, bologna, chicken, and jalapeno cheddar sausage, sides include homemade mac-cheese, potato salad, cowboy beans, baked beans, coleslaw, cornbread, and cobbler, with weekly specials. We have plenty of room for parking, if you drive an eighteen-wheeler or a motorcycle there is a place for you to park and for those who fit you can enjoy the convenience of our drive trough. We also have indoor or outdoor dining.
All our high qualitiy meat is purchased from local ranchers here in Oklahoma. One of our main beef suppliers is Goodsell Land and Cattle, they raise American & Fullblood Wagyu which is USDA Certified Prime. Wagyu is a Japanese cattle breed originally imported to the United States in 1975 For more information about Wagyu you can go to Goodsell Land and Cattle
